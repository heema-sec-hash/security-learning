* Single Quotes vs. Double Quotes

Since we are focusing on Python for your automation and security tools:

&#x20;- They are interchangeable for text.

&#x20;- Use whichever makes your code easier to read.

&#x20;- Just don't mix them up in the same sentence unless you need to nest them.
